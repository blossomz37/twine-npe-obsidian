### 1. Markdown to EPUB Converter
**Goal:** Convert your manuscript to e-book format

**Ask your AI:**

### 2. Batch PDF Converter
**Goal:** Convert multiple markdown files to PDF

**Ask your AI:**
> "Help me modify the batch converter to output PDF instead of DOCX."

**Key features to add:**
- Page headers/footers
- Custom fonts
- Table of contents
- Page numbers
