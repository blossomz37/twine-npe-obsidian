## Summary
The epilogue. Kai brings Freddy to the restored grotto. The Shell pulses with dual light—guardian blue and something new, something warm. Freddy belongs here now. They both do. The future stretches ahead like the tide: constant, changing, home.

- Kai shows Freddy the Shell—it pulses with new color, amber mixed with blue

- Final image: two figures in the glow, tide rising around them, together
